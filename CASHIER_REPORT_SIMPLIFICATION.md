# Cashier Report Simplification - Removed Floor Breakdown

## Change Made

Removed the floor/location breakdown from the cashier revenue display to show only the total revenue for each cashier.

## Before (Complex)
```
CASHIER CONTRIBUTIONS

• GROUND FLOOR CASHIER 1                    200,950 Br
  72.0% of total revenue
  GROUND: 196,930 Br    I5: 4,930 Br

• FLOOR 1(SOSNA ABERA) CASHER 2             78,130 Br
  28.0% of total revenue
  I1: 64,430 Br    I2: 13,690 Br
```

## After (Simple & Clear)
```
CASHIER CONTRIBUTIONS

• GROUND FLOOR CASHIER 1                    200,950 Br
  72.0% of total revenue

• FLOOR 1(SOSNA ABERA) CASHER 2             78,130 Br
  28.0% of total revenue
```

## What Changed

### Removed:
- ❌ Floor/location breakdown (GROUND: 196,930 Br, I5: 4,930 Br)
- ❌ Detailed floor labels
- ❌ Floor-specific revenue amounts

### Kept:
- ✅ Cashier name
- ✅ Total revenue amount
- ✅ Percentage of total revenue

## Files Modified

### app/admin/reports/page.tsx

**Change 1: Dashboard Display (Line 695-710)**
- Removed the breakdown display section
- Now shows only total and percentage

**Change 2: Export Function (Line 270-276)**
- Removed floor breakdown from description
- Simplified to just cashier name
